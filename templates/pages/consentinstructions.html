Instructions for People Under 18 Years Old

  1. Go to the Consent Form Link.
  2. Enter your name and email address under "Student".
  3. Enter your parent/guardian's name and email under "Parent/Guardian".
  4. Press "I agree to use electronic records and signatures." and then press "Continue."
  5. Read the Consent Form.
  6. Scroll down to the bottom of the Consent Form and click "Sign".
  7. Choose "Select Style."
  8. Press "Adopt and Sign" and then press "Finish."
  9. You can now close out the docusign.com page.
  10. Have your parent/guardian log into their email that you entered earlier and click on the email they received from DocuSign.
  11. Have your parent/guardian click "Review Document."
  12. Have your parent/guardian repeat steps 4-8.
  13. You can now close out the docusign.com page and begin the test when instructed by your teacher to do so.
